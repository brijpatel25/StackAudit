## FAQ — 5 Real Q&As

**Q: Is this actually free?**
Yes. The audit runs immediately with no account, no credit card, no catch. Email is optional and only asked after you've seen your results.

**Q: How do you know my spend data is accurate?**
You enter it. We don't have access to your billing. The audit engine uses your inputs and our pricing database — which we verify weekly against official vendor pages. If your actual plan costs differ from the listed price (e.g. annual billing, negotiated rates), adjust the monthly spend field manually.

**Q: What do you do with my email?**
We send you a copy of your audit. For cases with $500+/mo in savings, a Credex advisor may reach out to discuss how to capture those savings through AI credit strategies. We don't sell your data, and you can unsubscribe from any email.

**Q: Is the advice actually reliable, or is this just AI guessing?**
The audit engine is hardcoded rules, not AI. A human engineer wrote every recommendation rule based on actual plan feature differences and published pricing. The logic is documented and auditable. We use AI only to generate the summary paragraph — not the numbers or recommendations.

**Q: What if I'm already well-optimized?**
That's a valid result. The audit tells you clearly: "You're spending well." We don't manufacture fake savings. If you're already on the right plans for your usage, we'll say so — and offer to notify you when pricing or features change in ways that create new optimization opportunities.
